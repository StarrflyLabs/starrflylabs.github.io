What We Build

Science, in the hands of everyone.

We build tools that let ordinary people contribute to the scientific record — because the most important environmental data often lives in a backyard, not a lab. Our products share a common model: citizens contribute observations, we own the aggregation layer, and the dataset grows more valuable with every contribution.

Why We Exist

Our Mission

Starrfly Labs sees what others overlook — the living world beneath the soil, the invasive threat crossing the county line — and builds the tools to act on it.

The environmental data that matters most is often the hardest to collect. Satellites don't see soil microbiomes. Surveys don't move fast enough to catch an invasive species before it spreads. Government agencies have the mandate but not the infrastructure.

We fill that gap. Our products are designed from the ground up for citizen participation — simple enough for anyone to use, rigorous enough for researchers and institutions to trust. The data belongs to the community that generates it.

  • 01

    Open by Default

    The data should be as open as possible and as protected as necessary.

  • 02

    Rigor Without Gatekeeping

    A backyard sample is as scientifically valuable as a field study sample. We design for both.

  • 03

    Depth Over Breadth

    We build things that last. One good product is worth more than three half-finished ones.

About Starrfly Labs

Independent. Purposeful.

Starrfly Labs is an independent product studio based in North Carolina. We are building environmental intelligence products that didn't exist before — and that the world increasingly needs.

We are small by design. Every product we build gets our full attention until it works. We move carefully and we move with purpose.
